About

Cycling Benefits

Cycling Very Good our Health.

This group is for Dhaka Cyclist Club and it's surrounding Neighborhood. We're a group of people who are passionate about cycling. we envision our group as community to promoting cycling among the people of Dhaka City.

We are general people like you who is either a student, a service holder, a business person having a common goal of staying healthy in this stressful Dhaka City life and ride for recreation, commute, health, and sheer fun.
